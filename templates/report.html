Opportunities

What do these scores mean?
  • Strategic Fit (0–5): How directly the opportunity matches a SpatioX product capability.
  • Revenue Potential (0–5): Estimated deal size / business value if pursued.
  • Win Probability (0–5): How likely Silversea is to win given known relationships and competition.
  • Urgency (0–5): How time-sensitive the opportunity is (deadline proximity, competitive pressure).
  • Intelligence Quality (0–5): How well-grounded the signal is (named entities, dates, amounts vs. vague mentions).
  • Total Score (0–25): Sum of the five sub-scores. 20–25 = High  |  13–19 = Medium  |  0–12 = Low.

Note: the relevance gate and sector-categorization logic that produces these scores had two known issues as of 2026-06-24 (see STATE.md) — a fix has been applied but not yet verified with a live run. Scores reflect the analyst's intended scoring rubric; treat current live output as provisional until the next verified run.
